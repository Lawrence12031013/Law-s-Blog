About Me

This is what I do.

Hi, I'm Lawrence, a Front-End developer. At work, I primarily use Vue.js. In addition to Vue, I have experience with React, Node.js, and MongoDB. Recently, I started learning Python, which I find to be a really fun programming language. I'm very interested in it! I plan to share what I learn and what I am confused about on my blog, which serves as my personal notes. Maybe someday it will help someone like me.

I obtained my undergraduate degree in Communication Art and was a Graphic Designer. My strengths also include design, 3D modeling, and film making, which I now use for UI design. It helps a lot.

Learning is a long and steep road, like climbing mountains. I should work hard for seeing the beautiful views.
